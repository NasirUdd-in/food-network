Cheesy Tortilla Wrap

Paneer Badami

Paneer Badami is an appetizing Paneer Curry dish that is made from almonds, tomatoes, and lots of spices. If you're bored of eating other Paneer recipes, try giving this incredible main course recipe a chance to shine and fulfill your hunger pangs. Paneer Badami gets its name from the highlight of the recipe i.e., almonds.

Angara Paneer Masala

Angara Paneer Masala is a smoky paneer dish prepared from whole and powdered spices. It also has fresh cream in it to give that extra creamy flavor. Unlike other paneer recipes, this recipe is made with grilled paneer, which is added to the gravy. You will surely enjoy this mouth-watering dish.
